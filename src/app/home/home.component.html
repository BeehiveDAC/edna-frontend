What is Staking?
Staking is easiest to picture is you think of is similar to opening a Certificate of Deposit (CD) at your local bank.

Your choice of 3 "terms lengths" are offered: Weekly, Monthly or Quarterly.

Monthly is rewarded at 1.5 times the weekly reward, and quarterly at double the weekly "term".

There are no minimums or maximum stakes and you can withdraw at any time. You will be warned by the software if you will loose rewards when withdrawing early.


You will need Scatter to interact with the program managing contract.
